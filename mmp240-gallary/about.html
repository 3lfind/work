the others in Art

about us

The Others in Art was founded in 2016 as an educational web gallery by students at the Borough of Manhattan Community College. Central to its mission is to encourage an ever deeper understanding and enjoyment of art of the past and the present created by a diverse local, national and international audience that it serves and represents.

We hope to foster and continue to be there for the people who love art and design, create it and tinker with it. Art for the people by the people.
